About Micro Fire Engineers

Our History

Micro Fire Engineers (Pvt) Ltd was established in 2004 in Zimbabwe, bringing professional fire safety solutions to the region. Since our inception, we have grown to become a leading provider of comprehensive fire safety services.

What We Do

We specialize in comprehensive fire safety solutions including:

  • Fire Prevention Systems
  • Fire Protection Equipment
  • Fire Detection Technologies
  • Equipment Sales and Distribution
  • Professional Maintenance Services

Our Team

At Micro Fire Engineers, we take pride in our highly skilled team of management professionals and qualified technicians. Our dedication to quality service has helped us build a diverse client base that includes:

  • Individual Property Owners
  • Domestic Customers
  • Commercial Property Managers
  • Construction Contractors
  • Local Authorities

Our Commitment

We are committed to maintaining international standards in all our operations, ensuring that every client receives the highest quality fire safety solutions tailored to their specific needs.
